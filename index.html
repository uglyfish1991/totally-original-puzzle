Uni Buddy Word Puzzle

The rules!

Use your keyboard or the on screen keyboard to guess the secret 5-letter word hiding under the tiles. You've got 6 guesses. Pay attention to the colour of the borders, they're there to help:

  • 🟩 Green border: Nailed it. Right letter, right spot.
  • 🟨 Yellow border: Almost! The letter is in the word, just not at that spot.
  • ⬛ Black border: Nope. That letter didn't get the grades to join in.

Letters can show up more than once — just to keep things fun.

🔍Each week has a theme! See if you can spot them!

Good luck!
